## General Market Trends for Future Market-Trends Page

- Agent workspaces are becoming a product category: MagicPath and Appshots both point toward shared context surfaces where humans and agents coordinate work.
- Agent operations and security are merging: MCP sessions, credentials, tool configs, browser/editor context, and run recovery increasingly belong to one operational layer.
- Evaluation is becoming workflow infrastructure: LLM teams need regression capture, evidence, methodology, and plain-English reports, not isolated benchmark scores.
- Usage pricing creates a back-office wedge: AI/API companies keep creating billing, credits, reconciliation, and dispute workflows as pricing gets more granular.
- Enterprise readiness is still a small-team tax: SOC2, SSO, privacy review, and procurement evidence remain recurring friction for startups selling into larger accounts.
