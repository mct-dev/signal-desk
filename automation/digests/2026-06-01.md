- Score: 90

- Why now: this remains the densest buyer-pain cluster, with 27 pain signals and 36 spend signals around credits, invoice reconciliation, subscriptions, and usage tiers

### 2. LLM Evaluation Regression Workflows

- Score: 90

- Wedge: small-team eval runner that turns production failures into regression suites with plain-language reports
- Why now: evidence spans benchmark failures, red-team failures, package adoption, and teams looking for quality checks beyond raw model metrics
- Validation: collect 15 examples of teams reverting prompts or models because tests missed a regression

### 3. AI Agent Operations and Recovery

- Score: 90

- Why now: MCP session drops, public agent-governance seeds, and package adoption continue to point toward runtime supervision and recovery as the buying surface

### 4. Auth and SSO Integration Debugging

## General Market Trends for Future Market-Trends Page
